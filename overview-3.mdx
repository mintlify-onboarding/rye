A simple integration with Rye to:

* Gain instant access to 500+ million products to expand and diversify your inventory
* Order from any global brands on Shopify and Amazon to cater to all your gifters and recipients' desires
* Streamline operations and cut cost by eliminating individual connections and integrations with each merchants
* Accelerate and optimize the in-app checkout experience without redirects
  
This integration is suitable for any platform looking to build and expand their commerce experience.

Before you get started, make sure to [log in or register for a Rye account](https://console.rye.com/) on the Rye console.
